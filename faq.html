Parking
    Reserved Parking
  • 2 Stalls
    • The stalls at the front of the bottom lot near the mailboxes marked AXIS 45 with blue cones are ours and enforced. The map pin above goes directly there.

    • If we park well we can fit at least 5 in the driveway. We recommend reversing in if possible.
    Street Parking
  • 230th St SW
    • Immediately exiting our lot to the left, there are usually a few open spots along 230th.
  • 76th Ave W
    • From 230th st, take a left at the stop sign, and there should be plenty of marked street parking on the right. Past the 228th stoplight, there’s marked parking on both sides.
    • At the furthest point, it’s .25mi (5m walk, we’ve checked). any further and you’re at ranch99.
    • You can turn right onto 76th as well, but historically, you'll be walking much further.
