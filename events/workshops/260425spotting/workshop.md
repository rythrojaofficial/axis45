## Talk about assuming instruction
1. Spotting is like a partner lift in acro or dance: 
    1. the spotter is the lifter
    1. the spottee is the flyer
    1. both the spotter and spottee have a role to play
1. today we will assume certain instructions that are heavily emphasized in class so we can get to the actual techniques.
