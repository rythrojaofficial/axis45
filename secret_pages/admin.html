Opening & Closing

Opening Checklist
    Arrive Early
  • ~10m early is plenty
  • if you're running late, let the group chat(s) know
    Parking/Cones:
  • Park where you can, move cones if out
  • If cars are blocking stalls, check with APM
  • If cars are in or blocking the driveway, check with Sonderklasse, then APM
    • If there's nothing you can do, just alert the peeps in the chat(s)
    Turn On:
  • Lights
    • Garage/lobby lights only as necessary
      • garage panel: switch "2"
  • Powerstrip/Computer/Music as necessary
  • Fan/heater as necessary
    Check/Organize:
  • Leak. Check if there's new drops at or outside the normal spot
    • Take a pic if there is
  • Organize
    • Phone stands
    • Mats
    • Weights/balls/rollers/spinner/timers
  • Check and replenish if necessary/take note if we're running low
    • Paper towels
    • Toilet Paper
    • Soap
  • Sweep/Vacuum if necessary
Closing Checklist
    Prepare for close
  • Change the music
    • As sesh winds down, bring peeps into parasympathetic state unless you want to stay late
    • Either background beats, warm jazz, glitchxcity, or whatever else you like
  • Turn off heaters, extra fans
  • Garage light/lock garage
    Check/Organize:
  • Check Leak
    • Take a pic if needed
    • Bucket if needed
  • Organize
    • Phone stands
    • Mats
    • Weights/balls/rollers/spinner
  • Check
    • Paper towels
    • TP
    • Soap
  • Sweep/vacuum as needed
    Shutdown
  • Computer
  • Power Strip
    Make sure you're the last out
  • Lights off
  • Lock Door
    Cones
  • No driveway cones
  • One cone between the top spots
  • You can delegate if someones in those spots
Closing Checklist
    New people
  • Greet and chat
    • Where they're from
    • How they found us
    • How long they've been tricking
    • Have they done the waiver?
      • Waiver on the wall or website
    Tally
  • Tally near end of sesh
    • Admin Page for all things tally
  • check member status if unsure if member
    Non-members
  • If they're not on the tally list and you recognize them:
    • charge drop-in fee
    • if no card on file, try to get a card on file
    • if they only have mobile pay, just normal charge
  • If they're not on the tally list and you don't recognize them:
    • Ask them if they want to do membership
      • memberships for more deets
    • Or do drop in fee as above
  • Don't charge if:
    • They only came to hang out
    • They get injured
  • If there's a LOT of People (like 7+)
    • Write everyone's name down on a post it
    • check them off as you tally/charge so we don't duplicate/miss

Injury/Emergency Checklist P2
    Concious but it's BAD BAD
  • (Clear bone break, dislocation, usually screaming involved)
  • stay calm, slow your breathing
  • tell them to stay calm, focus on breathing, slow their breathing
  • put someone in charge of sesh, clear space
    • if no one trustworthy, cancel sesh, ask people to clear out, they won't be charged
  • if they can move, try to move off the floor otherwise clear space
  • ask whether they want a ride to the ER or an ambulance, or to be driven home by their Emergency contact/someone from sesh/uber/you
  • if they want an ambulance, or you to take them to the ER, cancel practice, ask people to clear out, they won't be charged
  • if need an ambulance, call 911
    • There has been this type of emergency _______
      • open fracture upper/lower arm/leg, etc
        • bone clearly broken, you might see bone
      • closed fracture upper/lower arm/leg, etc
        • bone clearly broken, but appears contained
      • dislocation of shoulder/collarbone/ankle/knee/kneecap
    • At our acrobatic martial arts studio Axis 45
      • 7701 230th St SW, Edmonds, WA 98026
    • The time is _____
    • Write everything down
    • log down how they physically left
