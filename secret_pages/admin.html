    Unconcious/Can't breathe/ Anything with Neck/Back
  • Put someone in charge of sesh, clear space
    • if no one trustworthy, cancel sesh, ask people to clear out, they won't be charged
    • if they can be safely moved, move off of floor, if not, clear space around them
    • call 911
      • There has been this type of emergency _______
        • anaphalactic shock
        • Seizure
        • neck/back injury
      • at our acrobatic martial arts studio Axis 45
        • 7701 230th St SW, Edmonds, WA 98026
        • The time is ______
        • Write these down
    • Alert emergency contact in waiver responses
      • original waiver
      • gmail search for their name or jotform
      • hi this is _____ Axis 45, Seattle Tricking and Acrobatics. ___ listed you as his emergency contact so we wanted to inform you that _____ has happened. We've taken these steps ______ and you can meet us here or at _____.
      • Log how they phsically left
        • drove home
        • ambulance
        • friend pickup
        • uber
      • Write down who and when you contacted with what info in this incident report form
Injury/Emergency Checklist P2
    Conscious but it's BAD BAD
  • (Clear bone break, dislocation, usually screaming involved)
  • stay calm, slow your breathing
  • tell them to stay calm, focus on breathing, slow their breathing
  • put someone in charge of sesh, clear space
    • if no one trustworthy, cancel sesh, ask people to clear out, they won't be charged
  • if they can move, try to move off the floor otherwise clear space
  • ask whether they want a ride to the ER or an ambulance, or to be driven home by their Emergency contact/someone from sesh/uber/you
  • if they want an ambulance, or you to take them to the ER, cancel practice, ask people to clear out, they won't be charged
  • if need an ambulance, call 911
    • There has been this type of emergency _______
      • open fracture upper/lower arm/leg, etc
        • bone clearly broken, you might see bone
      • closed fracture upper/lower arm/leg, etc
        • bone clearly broken, but appears contained
      • dislocation of shoulder/collarbone/ankle/knee/kneecap
    • At our acrobatic martial arts studio Axis 45
      • 7701 230th St SW, Edmonds, WA 98026
    • The time is _____
    • Write everything down in this incident report form
    • log down how they physically left
Injury/Emergency Checklist P3
    Conscious and it's a bummer
  • But not bad bad (sprains and below, ligament stuff, shoulder dislocations where they pop it back in)
  • help them move to the side and use blocks as necessary for them to be comfortable
  • ice/bandage if wanted. ice packs in the freezer in the middle room. Band aids are in blue box under empower banner
  • ask whether they can drive.
    • if no, ask if they want a ride to the ER, an ambulance, or to be driven home by their Emergency contact/someone from sesh/uber/you
  • write these down
  • log down how they physically left
