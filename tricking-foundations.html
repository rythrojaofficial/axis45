What is Tricking Foundations?

Tricking Foundations is our introductory class covering all bases in regards to understanding what tricking is.

We go over tricking takeoffs, set-ups, and shape variations for vertical (upright) and basic inverted acrobatic tricks. Students will use the foam padded spring floor and blocks to practice the skill appropriate vertical and horizontal acrobatic tricks and combinations.

  • Basic Horizontal and Vertical tricks
  • Basic Take-offs
  • Basic Setups
  • Basic Combo structure
  • Basic Shapes of tricking
    • Shuriken
    • Round
    • Doubleleg
    • Swipe
    • Hyperhook

If you want to learn just flips, you can also take our 7:30pm Flipping Progressions Class available for Drop-In.

Tricking Foundations can be taken in conjunction with the Flipping Progressions class for an additional discount
