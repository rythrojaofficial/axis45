AXIS 45

Axis 45 is Seattle's dedicated Tricking Studio owned and operated by Seattle Tricking featuring a 64ft long custom Spring Floor, AirTracks, mats, and more! We host dedicated open sessions for Trickers and breakdancers as well as one-on-one instruction, classes, workshops, events and more!

What is Tricking?

In three words, Tricking is Martial Arts Acrobatics. But let's go deeper.

Tricking is an Artistic Discipline that explores acrobatic movement through elements incorporated from Martial Arts, Tumbling, and Dance

If you want to learn more about Tricking, we go in depth in our Tricking Overview!
